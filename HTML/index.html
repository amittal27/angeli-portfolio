My name is Angeli Mittal. Studying neuroscience & computer science at Northwestern, I seek the intersection between engineering and medicine.

For the last year, I've conducted biomedical research at Lurie Children's Hospital in the Center for Autonomic Medicine in Pediatrics. My current project examines the mechanism for neurodevelopmental impairment in preterm infants experiencing abnormal cardiorespiratory episodes.

I enjoy teaching and volunteering, serving as a peer mentor for some of Northwestern's computer science classes. You can also find me volunteering at Hines VA Hospital's emergency department with my EMT training or at the pediatric floor of Shirley Ryan AbilityLab.

I'm also interested in public health and design. I'm the Digital Managing Editor at The Daily Northwestern — my college newspaper — as well as the campus and city public health reporter. At OM, Northwestern's Hindu student association, I serve as the treasurer.
